Product Range

Electric Motor Scrap

Consists of various grades of ferrous and non-ferrous metals recovered from disassembled electric motors. May include copper windings, steel housings, aluminum components, and potentially precious metals.

Copper Ingots

Small, rectangular blocks of high-purity copper, often with a distinctive reddish-orange color. Produced through the refining and casting of scrap copper.

Brass Scrap Honey

High-purity brass scrap consisting of solids and turnings, free from excessive dirt, oil, and other contaminants. Typically boasts a bright yellow-gold color.

Heavy Melting Steel

Heavy Melting Steel scrap, a mix of HMS 1 and HMS 2 grades. HMS 1 is clean steel free of contaminants. HMS 2 may contain some light non-ferrous attachments.

Copper Billets

Solid blocks of high-purity copper, often cylindrical in shape. Typically produced through the refining and casting of scrap copper.

Aluminium Scrap Wheels

Scrap aluminum sourced from discarded car and truck wheels. May consist of various alloy grades with varying levels of magnesium and silicon content.

Brass billets

Solid brass blocks, typically cylindrical or rectangular, made from refined brass scrap. Used in machining and manufacturing applications.

Aluminum ingots

Rectangular aluminum blocks produced by smelting scrap or virgin metal. Commonly used in casting, rolling, and extrusion processes.
